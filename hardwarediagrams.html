Hardware Identification Chart

PCI

It can record things on the screen, play TV on the computer and record audio. Many people who use youtube or watch TV on their computer must have this

Power Supply

A power supply unit (PSU) converts mains AC to low-voltage regulated DC power for the internal components of a computer.

Graphics Card

The graphics card in a computer takes over processing when the micro-processor is busy doing other things.

RAM

RAM stands for random access memory. What RAM does is it acts as temporary storage for things that are not stored in the Harddrive.

Hard Drive

The hard drive is used for storing and recieving permanent information and files.
